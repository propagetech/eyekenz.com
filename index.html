Welcome to Eyekenz z Healthcare

Eyekenz z Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. has successfully completed 5 years of dedicated service in the field of ophthalmology, empowering healthcare professionals with both clinical and non-clinical products that combine quality, innovation, and affordability.
With deep-rooted expertise in ophthalmic services, Eyekenz z has built a reputation for meeting the diverse and evolving needs of clinicians and healthcare administrators. Our broad product portfolio and on-time delivery, coupled with a personalized approach, support our "No Look Further" philosophy.
We are proud of our strong market presence, trusted relationships with eye care providers, and our deep understanding of the ophthalmology landscape. These strengths, along with strategic collaborations—including partnerships with global OEMs and reputed brands like HOYA—enable us to deliver a win-win outcome for all stakeholders.
Eyekenz z Healthcare remains committed to expanding access, elevating standards, and driving excellence in eye care delivery across India and beyond.
